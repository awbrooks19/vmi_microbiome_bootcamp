Welcome to the Second VMI Bootcamp!¶

VMI Website: Vanderbilt Microbiome Initiative.

  • Organized by the Vanderbilt Microbiome Initiative (VMI) and funded by Vanderbilt University Trans-Institutional Programs (TIPs).
  • Founded by members of the Dept. Biological Sciences, Peabody college, School of Engineering and the Medical center
  • Research – 120 Vanderbilt scholars committed to fundamental and translational microbiome research
  • Members include undergraduates, graduate, postdocs, and faculty
  • Multidisciplinary community - Biological Sciences, Biostatistics, Cell and Developmental Biology, Chemistry, Medicine, Epidemiology, PMI, GHN, Vantage
  • Education and training – Campus-wide programs to deploy state-of-the-art tools to propel microbiome research
  • Ongoing Clinical Trial - Multi’omic, dietary intervention, temporal sampling, multi-ethnic

Microbiome Bootcamp¶

This workshop is a basic introduction into microbiome analysis

Goals:

  1. Understand the importance of good experimental design
  2. Explore the critical steps in a typical microbiome analysis
  3. Utilize the QIIME2 pipeline to process microbiome datasets
